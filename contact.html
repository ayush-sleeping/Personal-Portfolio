Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? Here are answers to some common inquiries about my work and services.

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in full-stack web development with Laravel for backend, JavaScript/ReactJS for frontend, and MySQL for databases. I also have experience with Git, AWS, Bootstrap, and modern web development practices.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple website takes 1-2 weeks, while complex web applications can take 4-8 weeks. I always provide detailed timelines during project planning and keep you updated throughout development.

Do you work on both frontend and backend?

Yes! I'm a full-stack developer with 1+ years of production experience. I can handle everything from database design and API development to responsive UI/UX implementation, giving you a complete solution from a single developer.

What's your development process like?

I follow a structured approach: 1) Requirements gathering, 2) Planning & design, 3) Development with regular updates, 4) Testing & debugging, and 5) Deployment & support. I use Git for version control and provide regular progress updates.

Are you available for remote work?

Absolutely! I'm based in Mumbai, India and work with clients globally. I'm comfortable with remote collaboration using tools like Slack, Zoom, and project management platforms. I can adapt to different time zones for meetings and communication.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, I provide post-deployment support including bug fixes, minor updates, and guidance on using your new application. I also offer ongoing maintenance packages for regular updates, security patches, and feature enhancements.
